**To the Editorial Board,**

We are pleased to submit our manuscript, *"A Unified Geometric Resolution of the Hubble Tension via Logarithmic Expansion Drift and Half-Turn Topology,"* for consideration as a Research Article in Physical Review D.

Our work provides a primary geometric resolution to the 5-sigma Hubble Tension. By demonstrating a statistically consistent Logarithmic Expansion Drift (=2.1$), we reconcile local $ measurements with the Planck acoustic scale. We believe this work offers a falsifiable and elegant solution to one of the most pressing problems in modern cosmology.
